🎯 The One Track Concept

Every user gets ONE track per song version. This makes every contribution special and valuable. Together, users create complete songs by adding their unique parts!

1. Create or Select a Song

Browse the song library or create your own song to get started. Each song starts with a creator and grows as others contribute.

2. Add Your ONE Track

Click Record to hear all existing tracks while you record your part. Once you add your track, you've made your mark on that version!

3. Create Forks for New Directions

Want to take a song in a new direction? Click "Fork This Version" to create your own version where YOU control the BPM and direction.

4. Collaborate & Engage

Vote on tracks you love, comment to give feedback, and message other users to collaborate on new musical ideas!
